This is an Arabic dish called "Maqluba," which translates to "upside down." It's made with delicious layers of spiced meat, vegetables, and rice in a pot, which is flipped over onto a large serving platter and everybody helps themselves.


Ingredient

  • 7 cups water
  • 1 Onion and 1 garlic
  • tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • and more

Steps

  1. Place water, onion, garlic, cinnamon, turmeric, garam masala, salt, and pepper in a large pot
  2. Heat cooking oil in a large, deep skillet over medium heat
  3. Layer lamb in the bottom of the large pot
  4. Cover the pot and simmer over low heat until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed, 30 to 45 minutes.
